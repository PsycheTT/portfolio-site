Hi! I'm Emilie

Graphic Designer and Artist

Emilie Jane is an artist, graphic designer, and globetrotter. Born in Trinidad and Tobago, Emilie migrated to the US in her early teens. A few years later, she moved to the UK, then Canada, then France, before finally returning to Trinidad in her late teens. She is a self-proclaimed 'roving artist' with a style that reflects her love of travel, people, and art.
